About Amma's Ark

Welcome to Amma's Ark

A Place to Learn, Explore and Grow. Amma's Ark is a modern activity centre where children develop confidence, creativity and life skills through engaging experiences that inspire lifelong learning.

Today, our centre offers thoughtfully designed programs in chess, art, dance, yoga, karate, public speaking, storytelling and calligraphy. As Amma's Ark continues to grow, our vision is to introduce more indoor and outdoor activities, sports, creative programs and enrichment experiences that help every child discover their full potential.
